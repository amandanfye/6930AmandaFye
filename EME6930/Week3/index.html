About

Amanda was born and raised in Detroit, Michigan where she grew up with three younger brothers. Throughout her childhood she trained in all areas of dance from Russian ballet to hip-hop. As a high school student, Amanda was a member of a nationally ranked, competitive, all-star team. Senior year Amanda and her team took home the gold medal in pom and a silver medal in jazz at the USASF Dance Worlds. After graduating high school, Amanda continued her dance career by becoming a member of a collegiate dance team, teaching summer camps through Universal Dance Association, and coaching younger students in all areas of dance.

Amanda attended Central Michigan University where she earned a Bachelors of Science in Secondary Education. Her major was Integrated Science and Amanda earned a double-minor in Earth Science and Middle Level Education. Throughout her time at Central Michigan, she was also a member of the dance team, and eventually became a captain and then a coach.

After graduating from Central Michigan, Amanda moved to Tampa, Florida to take her first teaching job as a middle school science teacher at Safety Harbor Middle School in Pinellas County, Florida. After two years, Amanda took a job as the Varsity Dance Coach at Plant High School in Tampa, Florida. She was then offered a Biology teaching position at Plant High where she taught for three years. During this time, Amanda grew interested in learning about instructional design and online education. She enrolled at the University of South Florida in the Masters of Science in Learning Design and Technology Program.

Interested in instructional design, Amanda began looking for jobs where her creative spirit and passion for design and technology could take center stage. She landed an instructional design job at Moffitt Cancer Center in Tampa, Florida. Currently, Amanda works as a Curriculum Development Specialist in Revenue Cycle Quality and Training. Amanda will graduate in May 2020 with a Masters of Science in Learning Design and Technology, a graduate certificate in E-Learning Design and Development, and a graduate certificate in Web Design. Amanda hopes to continue to learn and grow in the field of instructional design after graduation through her job at Moffitt.
